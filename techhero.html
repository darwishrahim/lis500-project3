Tech Heroes

Maxine Williams & Elizabeth Nyamwange

Maxine Williams

Maxine Williams, Facebook’s Global Chief Diversity Officer, is a key figure in the push for greater diversity in the tech industry. She is known for her straightforwardness and strategic approach, making her a force in the diversity and inclusion space.

As one of Mark Zuckerberg and Sheryl Sandberg’s go-to executives, Williams has played a major role in Facebook’s diversity efforts. According to Facebook’s 2017 diversity update:

  • The number of women working at Facebook globally increased from 33% to 35%.
  • Women in technical roles rose from 17% to 19%.
  • Hispanic representation in the U.S. workforce increased from 4% to 5%.
  • Black representation grew from 2% to 3%.

Despite these improvements, Williams believes there is still a long way to go. “If you ask me when would I be satisfied, I’d be satisfied when it’s 50% [women at Facebook],” she told Glassdoor. “More, I always want more.”

Her mission goes beyond just increasing numbers—she wants Facebook’s workforce to truly reflect its over two billion users worldwide.

Elizabeth Nyamwange

Elizabeth Nyamwange is a young entrepreneur and inventor. She's the founder and creator of Etana, a solar-powered fingerprint scanning device that allows users to create uniqe biometric digital identification without relying on internet access. This invention addresses a critical need for digital identification in low-infrastructure environments, empowering women and enhancing their access to essential services.

She is currently a student at the Illinois Math and Science Academy, where she is deeply involved in tech, science, and art, with a particular focus on gender issues.

In 2021, Elizabeth began programming the software for Etana, and by 2022, she had incorporated her project and raised $500,000 for a pilot program. Her work has garnered significant attention and support, including collaborations with Safaricom and Visa. Elizabeth also serves as a youth advisor for the United Nations on gender and education, further amplifying her impact on a global scale.

Elizabeth's dedication to her cause and her innovative spirit have earned her numerous accolades, including recognition from MIT Solve and various media outlets. Her journey is a testament to the power of combining technical skills with a deep commitment to social justice, and she continues to inspire others with her vision and determination.
